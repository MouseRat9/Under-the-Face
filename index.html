Under the Face is a Survival horror game, try to survive and do die, try to understand and will mad, try escape and get lost.

UNDER THE FACE

Enviroment:

The game takes place in a village lost in the middle of the forest, where the fog never ends and time never passes, whether for the weather, but also for its inhabitants.

Gameplay:

explore and village during day and sleep in nihgt, if you'll can, protect your base and craft traps, help the villagers and obtain important resourcers for you journey to escape where, and fight if it's really be necessary.

Steath:

Try to make as little noise as possible, hide in objects or in the shadows to monsters, and use tricks to distance them and throw them off track.

Resource Management

combine items and disassemble others to obtain parts and new effects and results, take care of your items meticulously as they are scarce and at other times "only ones".

Puzzles:

Many objects in the game have passwords or puzzles to be discovered for progression, always stay alert, the hints will always be there
